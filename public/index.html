      Community rules

      2Z0 Chatroom is a friendly place to meet people who share your interests. To keep it that way, everyone agrees to a few rules. The system auto-enforces most of these.

      1. Be kind. No harassment, hate speech, slurs, or targeted attacks of any kind.
      2. Keep it clean. Inappropriate, sexual, or violent content is not allowed. Profanity is auto-blocked.
      3. No links or contact info. URLs, bare domains, IPs, Discord/Telegram invites, and emails are auto-blocked to prevent phishing and spam.
      4. Don't spam. Rapid or repeated messages trigger a cooldown. Repeated flooding results in an automatic mute.
      5. Don't impersonate. Don't pretend to be someone else or misrepresent your identity.
      6. No personal info. Don't share your (or anyone else's) full name, address, phone, email, school, or workplace.
      7. Respect everyone. Different opinions are fine — personal attacks are not. Keep debates civil.
      8. Report issues. Leave any room that makes you uncomfortable. Messages are ephemeral and not stored.

      How auto-moderation works

      • Profanity filter on nicknames, interests, and messages
      • Link, domain, IP, and contact-info detection
      • Flood control (max 8 messages / 10 seconds)
      • Duplicate-message blocking
      • Repeated-character spam blocking
      • Auto-mute (60s) after repeat offenses
      • Per-IP connection limits

      By using 2Z0 Chatroom you agree to these rules. Violating them may result in being muted or disconnected.
